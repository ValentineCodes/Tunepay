How much does it cost to use Storefront?

Paystack Storefront is completely free to use, and there are no setup fees, or subscription fees. We only charge a transaction fee on each successful sale, so as always, Paystack only makes money when you make money. This is our current pricing.

In which countries is Storefront available?

Paystack Storefront is enabled for all Paystack merchants in South Africa, Ghana, and Nigeria. To create your Storefront, start by creating your free Paystack account.

Can I sell digital products, such as ebooks or videos with Storefront?

Yes you can. You can set it up so that customers are redirected to your digital product after payment.
